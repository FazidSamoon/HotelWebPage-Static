Delux Lake View Room

Book This Room Type

Deluxe by name and by nature, the light filled, contemporary elegance of our Deluxe Lakeview Rooms make them a natural choice for guests seeking extra luxury and style during their stay in Killarney. Relax from the privacy of the spacious south-facing well equipped balcony, boasting stunning direct views over the manicured gardens, Lough Lein and MacGillycuddy’s Reeks.
Located within the main hotel building and thoughtfully furnished with every modern convenience, these large rooms feature rich wooden panelling, new multimedia system, wingback armchair and modern technologies.
Carefully chosen facilities ensuring the most comfortable stay are included, from electronic mini-bar, Nespresso machine, fluffy bathrobe and slippers and WiFi, to a multimedia system and complimentary access to environmentally friendly digital versions of 1,000s of newspapers & magazines via the Press Reader App and a SuitePad in-room tablet with Guest Directory & Digital Concierge.

At a Glance

  • 41 m2 / 441 Sq Ft
  • Spacious Bedroom with 1 Super King or Twin Beds (Two Singles)
  • Large Ensuite Bathroom with Separate Bath & Shower
